Once upon a time
I studied graphic design and then industrial design in the magical Florence. During the university I was selected twice to exhibit my works (in exhibitions and in magazines). In Berlin I won a graphic design competition for an Italian street festival. I got a scholarship for the polimi university in Milan.

Then
I fell in love with Berlin and I started to do internships between Italy and Berlin. Curiosity and passion led me to learn many different things (trademarks, logos, books and illustrations) and to have different experiences (agencies, start-ups, publishing house).

The DCI
I also worked as a freelancer and I loved working with my clients on their projects and helping them carry out their projects but... I wanted to learn coding and how to create digital UX/UI design projects. So in 2018 I started at DCI and so started my journey to the dci.
